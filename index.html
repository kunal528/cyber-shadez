Welcome to Cyber Shadez - 2023

GLS University - Faculty of Computer Applications & Information Technology and Faculty of Computer Technology are hosting International Webinar and Technical Festival “Cyber Shadez 2023” based on the theme of “Cyber Security”.
The objective of the TechFest is to provide a platform for the dynamic young students to showcase their technical skills through the events which will be conducted during the TechFest. It also intends to give the insight of how Cyber Security is going to impact the lives of people, indystries and society.
24 - 25 February, 2023

CYBER SECURITY

The Cyber Security technology aims at creating computational machines that have perceptual and sensory ability like those of human beings. It uses nonobtrusive sensing method, employing most modern video cameras and microphones to identify the users’ actions through the use of imparted sensory abilities.The machine can understand what a user wants, where he is looking at, and even realize his physical or emotional states. In the name of Cyber Security Blue in this term stands for Blue tooth (which enables wireless communication) and eyes because eye movement enables us to obtain a lot of interesting and information.
The TechFest provides a platform for the young talents have an insight of Cyber Security applications.

ABOUT US

GLS University
GLS University is established in the year of 2015 with the objective of providing an ideal and creative learning environment and continuing the tradition of excellence in education of the sponsoring body of the University, viz., Gujarat Law Society (GLS). Gujarat Law Society, established in the year 1927, is one of the largest and oldest educational institutions in the State of Gujarat. GLS was promoted by luminaries Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, Shri Ganesh Mavlankar, the first Speaker of the parliament and Shri Kasturbhai Lalbhai with the vision of Excellence in Education. Shri I. M. Nanavati steered the expansion of GLS into premier multidisciplinary institution. GLS university offers UG & PG programmes in several disciplines such as Computer Technology & Applications, Business Management, Commerce, Business Administration, Education, Design, Law etc.
Faculty of Computer Applications & IT
GLS University under its Faculty of Computer Applications and Information Technology offers courses like BCA, PGDCA, MSc (IT) and Integrated MSc (IT). GLS (I & R. K. Desai) Institute of Computer Applications( NAAC Accredited) started a BCA programme in 1999 with the mission of enabling IT professionals of tomorrow. All the programmes under Faculty of Computer Applications & IT are designed using Open Source as a delivery platform. The department offers best-in-class infrastructure with well-equipped computer laboratories. The MSc (IT) programme along with Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications (PGDCA) was started along with the inception of the university. The programme has been well accepted by the student fraternity. The fifth batch of MSc (IT) students has been also well accepted by IT Industry. GLSU has started a new five year Integrated MSc(IT) programme in the year 2016.
